~ stars smiled at me ~

I peaked outside from my window.
And saw my friends hanging out in the sky.
I observed from afar.
Wanting to join but I couldn't.
So I drew them down.
Though sometimes the clouds are in the way,
still,
I hope to capture this joyous moment.

Faith Yu
